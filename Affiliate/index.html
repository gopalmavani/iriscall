Use

Become a user of our products for your day to day activities, communication needs and more.

Invite

TPW is by invitation only.

We need to make sure that every customer that registers is linked to an existing customer. We also need to make sure that every customer you personally introduce to any of our products is linked to you. That's why all actions, all invites, ... have personalised invitation links.

Accept

Every brand has it's own unique features.

The TPW brands will make sure that the prospect understands what the invite is about and how the application can benefit him/her. This will be supported by information websites, marketing videos, tutorials, ... All remembering your invitation link.

Register

The only thing left to do is... register!

Just like for any application you've ever used, you need to do a standard registration. Once registered for this application, this person is now linked to you.

The user can generate a revenue from paid services, upsells or premium services like subscriptions.

Rewards

Earn credits or cash.

You are the reason this new member registered with any of the TPW applications, so you will be rewarded for any revenue generated by this user in this application. The rewards are paid in the form of credits or cash, depending on your community status.
